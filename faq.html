Questions

Do You Restore Old Pictures

Yes, we can help bring old photos back to life! Whether they're faded, torn, or damaged, we can restore them to their former glory, making them look as good as new. Just bring us your old pictures, and we'll work our magic to restore them for you.



How Do You Restore The Pictures

Restoring pictures using Adobe products is like giving a vintage photo a digital makeover! With Adobe software like Photoshop or Lightroom, we can fix old photos by adjusting colors, removing scratches or stains, and even reconstructing missing parts. It's like using advanced digital tools to breathe new life into your cherished memories. We can work together step by step to make sure the restored picture looks just the way you want it to!



How can we make an enquiry

Making an inquiry is simple! You can reach out to us by sending an email through the contact page on our website. Just fill out the form with your name, email address, and your message or question, and we'll get back to you as soon as possible. We're here to help with any inquiries you may have!



How Can I send you a picture I want Restored

Sending a picture for restoration is easy! You can either upload it directly on our website when you submit your request or email it to us at imageretold@gmail.com Whichever way works best for you, just make sure to include the picture you want to be restored, and we'll take it from there!
